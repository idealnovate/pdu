Scheme of Work

Elementary UI/UX Design
Bootcamp Curriculum

A structured, hands-on curriculum designed to take you from complete beginner to job-ready designer in 12 intensive weeks.

Programme Overview

12-week live online bootcamp designed for absolute beginners with zero tech background.

12Weeks
48+Live Sessions
10+Projects
5Tools Mastered

What's included:

  • Live instructor-led sessions
  • Weekly assignments & feedback
  • Portfolio project support
  • Global certificate upon completion
  • What is UI vs UX — understanding the difference and why both matter
  • Introduction to Design Thinking: Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test
  • Overview of the design industry: roles, career paths and earning potential
  • Setting up your Figma workspace and navigating the interface
  • Assignment: Create your first mood board and design brief
  • User research methods: interviews, surveys, observation, secondary research
  • Creating user personas and empathy maps from research data
  • Understanding user journeys and mapping pain points
  • Introduction to information architecture, sitemaps and user flows
  • Assignment: Conduct user interviews and build a persona document
  • Lo-fi and hi-fi wireframing techniques using Figma
  • Typography in UI: choosing fonts, scale, hierarchy and readability
  • Colour theory for digital design: palettes, accessibility, contrast ratios
  • Spacing, grids and layout systems (8-point grid)
  • Assignment: Design a 5-screen wireframe for your chosen app concept
  • Building reusable components: buttons, cards, navigation bars, forms
  • Figma Auto Layout: creating responsive, scalable components
  • Introduction to design systems and design tokens
  • Building a mini design system for your portfolio project
  • Assignment: Build a complete component library in Figma
  • Creating interactive prototypes in Figma with transitions and animations
  • Micro-interactions: hover states, loading states, feedback cues
  • Planning and conducting a usability test session
  • Analysing test results and iterating on your design
  • Assignment: Prototype your app and run a usability test with 3 users
  • Using Figma AI for auto-generation, content fill and design suggestions
  • FigJam AI for whiteboarding sessions and collaborative ideation
  • Leveraging Claude AI for UX writing, persona generation and documentation
  • Introduction to Lovable AI: turning Figma designs into live web prototypes
  • Assignment: Use AI tools to redesign and document an existing product screen
  • Structuring your Behance and portfolio case study for maximum impact
  • Writing compelling design case studies that get recruiter attention
  • Optimizing your LinkedIn profile and applying for design roles
  • Capstone project: end-to-end design challenge with live critique
  • Graduation ceremony, certificate issuance & community onboarding
